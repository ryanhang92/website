What interests me?

I combined stories that my parents passed down to me with my own reading of military history to form my first academic interests. In the future I hope to understand the causes of and solutions to conflict.

In this stage of my life I am trying to learn as much as I can about Math and Computer Science!

Convolutional Neural Nets

In the final course of Berkeley's introductory CS series I was able to achieve greater than a 3x performance speedup of a convolutional neural network trained to identify cats in images, without the use of multiple machines. This project allowed me to tie together my understanding of memory, cache usage, paralleism in computing, how C code is compiled into assembly, and other topics in lower-level computing.

Candidate Profiler

I built the first protoype of the Political Sentiment Analyzer during my work with Basic Ballot and this was my first attempt at applying rudimentary machine learning. This project applied Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) and text sentiment analyzers to dicover how candidates felt about political topics by parsing through a collections of news articles scraped from the web. This experience continues to drive my interst in applying Computer Science to political questions.

Map Alert

In response to the spike in crime near the area where I live, I created an incident map alert system with the MEAN Stack. This allows users to view and post public saftey alerts to the community, alerts can be queried and searched by attributes such as tag and time.

Study Hall

Driven by my classroom teaching experience, I am currently in the process of creating a reddit-esq classroom management tool. Built in Django, this application allows students to post questions and recieve feedback. Posts are displayed via a voting system and there are moderation controls for the content. Login and authenticaion features are supported and students are able to have their own personalized accounts.

Yumster

I am currently working with two of my friends on the front end and back end of a project to analyze food recipies and their ingredients through machine learning techniques. I help construct the site's layout and navigation system, data displays, login, and admin functionality.

To Do

To-do applications such as SimpleNote play a huge role in my life and drive my productivity. Inspired by the power of such small applications, I endeavored to create my own with the MEAN stack!

Kruskal's on a Graph

Upon finishing a course on Data Structures I was able to implement Kruskal's algorithm to find the minimum spanning tree of a graph. This project allowed me marry my knowledge data structures and algorithms and sparked my interest in problem solving through the lens of Computer Science.

Machine Learning

Following my interest in Data Science and Statistics I completed Andrew Ng's Machine Learning course on Coursera. This course of was one of the best learning experiences of my life and convinced me to the potential of MOOCs. In this course I implemented classic machine learning techniques such as Linear Regression, Logistics Regression, Neural Nets, SVMs, K-means, Collaborative Filtering...and the list goes on!
